About

Amel is a non-governmental Lebanese organization that was founded in 1979. We offered more than 8 million services to all in medical and other sectors.

Amel is a non-governmental organization, recognized as a public utility by presidential decree 5832 in 1994. Amel is a non-profit, non sectarian organization that supports the most underprivileged populations in Lebanon, through various programs involving health care, psychosocial support, rural development, vocational training, child protection and the promotion of human rights.
